Washington Slagbaai National Park

The Washington Slagbaai Nation Park is a 5600+ hectare nature sanctuary on the North end of the island. Rich in culture, history, and ecolology, this park offers a museum and hiking trails. Flamingos, parakeets, parots, iguanas, and many other species can be observed within the park.

Donkey Sanctuary Bonaire

The Donkey Sanctuary provides shelter and support to the many donkeys that live on the island. Take a tour around the sanctuary while curious donkeys come to greet you.
