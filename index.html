Imagine yourself coming home to a warm home knowing that the furnace kicked on in preparation for your return instead of leaving it on all day. Imagine moving freely throughout your house without worrying about turning lights on and off. Imagine saving hundreds of dollars every year while you reduce your carbon footprint. Smart home technology can provide countless benefits to those willing to take the initiative and improve themselves and their home. By incorporating this technology into your home you can create a reactive and connected system in which you will live.
