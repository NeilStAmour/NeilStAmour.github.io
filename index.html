The perfect vacation rental in beautiful Bonaire

Prime
Location

Located on the western coast of the beautiful and safe island of Bonaire, enjoy calm waters and beautiful sunsets. A short walk to ideal diving and snorkeling locations with beautiful scenery everywhere you look.

Private
Community

Small gated community located on the North end of the island, a scenic 10 minute drive from the city of Kralendijk. Enjoy a peaceful vacation at the villa or take a short trip down to the city for the nightlife of a Caribbean island.

Modern
Amenities

Brand new villa with a fully equipped open concept kitchen of new appliances, spacious accessible bathrooms, and individually air conditioned bedrooms. The villa includes WiFi, smart TV, and a Sonos sound system.

Private
Pool

Enjoy a private pool surrounded by tropical gardens and warm breezes in the comfort of your own private vacation home. The pool uses a magnesium mineral solution which avoids all the downsides of chlorine while providing health and ecological benefits.
