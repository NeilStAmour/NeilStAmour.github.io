Resources

EnerGuide Home Energy Evaluations

Energuide is a Canadian government company that provides users with an evaluation to determine the potential changes that they can make to their current home. This group focuses on energy efficiency and common modifications that can be made to increase heating and energy efficiency.

Canada Smart Home

Canada Smart Home is a commercial company dedicated to providing residential and commercial smart home solutions to those interested in building or modifying their own space.

Smart AV Inc

This is another company that provides home automation technology to its clients with a focus on audio/visual technology.

Self Improvement

For those who are comfortable making the changes by themselves many stores will have the resources you need. You will want to check at your local home renovation and technology stores to see what tools they have available.
